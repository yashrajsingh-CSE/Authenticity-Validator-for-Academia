## ✨ Features

- **🔐 Full Authentication Flow** – Login, Signup, Forgot Password with University SSO mock, social buttons, and form validation.
- **🧠 AI‑Powered Detection** – Sentence‑level analysis reveals **AI‑generated**, **mixed**, and **human‑written** content with confidence scores.
- **📊 Real‑Time Dashboards** – Interactive charts (line, doughnut, bar, radar) for submissions, plagiarism trends, and authenticity metrics.
- **📂 Drag‑and‑Drop Uploads** – Accepts PDF, DOCX, TXT, PPTX files with live scanning animations and queue management.
- **📑 Detailed Reports** – Per‑submission reports highlighting plagiarism percentage, citation issues, and overall risk level.
- **📚 Citation Validator** – Checks APA/MLA/Chicago formatting, verifies DOIs, and flags dead or suspicious links.
- **🔬 Research Integrity Tools** – Detects p‑hacking, duplicate publications, and image manipulation.
- **🏫 Institution Dashboard** – Enterprise views with department performance, faculty alerts, and leaderboards.
- **⚙️ Settings & Profile** – Editable profile, theme customization, and mock API configuration.
- **🌗 Dark/Light Mode** – Full theme toggle with persistent user preference.
- **📱 Fully Responsive** – Works flawlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile with a collapsible sidebar.
- **✨ Smooth Animations** – Page transitions, skeleton loaders, progress bars, and micro‑interactions.
